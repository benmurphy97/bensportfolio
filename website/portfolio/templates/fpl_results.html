Expected Standings

Expected points measure luck. In any gameweek, if you score the highest FPL points you earn 3 expected points because no one can beat you. If you rank 3rd in a 10-person league, you beat 7 but lose to 2 — so your expected points are (7/9) × 3 = 2.33. A positive +/- means you're outperforming your luck.
